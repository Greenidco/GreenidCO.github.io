The GREEN ID® Horizontal Scrubber or gas filter is used in many industrial applications. Its uses include the cleaning of fumes from plating ( PCB Plating ), anodizing, steel pickling, printed circuit board plating, semi-conductor clean rooms, and corrosive odor control units.


TREATED POLLUTANTS:


Alkaline: NaOH, NaClO3, …

Acids: HCl, HF, HCN, HBr, H3PO4, …

Sulfur compounds: H2SO4, SO2, SO3, H2S, Mercaptans, …

         Nitrogen compounds: NH3, NO2, RNH2, …

                                Toxic gases: Cl2, BF3, SO3, …

This is a cross flow design with scrubbing liquid flowing vertically downwards while the gas passes horizontally through the packing section. A Monroe Horizontal Packed Bed Scrubber is used when limited head room is an issue at a customer's facility or when low profile is desired for roof top applications.
