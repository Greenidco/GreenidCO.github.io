Screw conveyors are used for horizontal, inclined, or vertical transport of bulk and granulated materials.

GREEN ID Screw Conveyors are designed for inclined transport (up to 8˚) of material with temperatures below 300˚C. They have a conveying capacity up to 550 m^3/h and are manufactured up to 60 m length. A 0.30-0.35 degree of filling is recommended, depending on the material.

The screw pitch is individual for each of the ten conveyor sizes to attain lowest power consumption and smooth transport. The screw is completely enclosed, so the conveyance is weatherproof and dust-free.
